Who we are

One contractor for the whole project — from foundation to finish.

Founded in 2007 by Kaya Maraya Silkiss-Hero, Kare Kontracting was built on a simple idea: islanders deserve a single, reliable partner who can handle every piece of a project with care. From new construction and remodels to the plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and landscaping that finish them, we coordinate the whole job under one roof.

The result is fewer headaches, tighter timelines, and a finished space you genuinely love — backed by a personalized, hands-on follow-through you won’t find with the average contractor.

  • Licensed & bonded in Washington State
  • Bundle discounts on full-spectrum builds
