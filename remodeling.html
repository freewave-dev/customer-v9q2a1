Overview

Home remodels in the San Juan Islands.

Remodeling your home is one of the best ways to add a touch of style, class, and sophistication to your property — and to make it work better for the way you actually live. Since 2007, Kare Kontracting has handled full and partial remodels for island homeowners, from a single bathroom refresh to a top-to-bottom renovation.

We manage every layer of the project: fixtures, appliances, paint, and drywall, plus a wide range of tile options and interior finishes. When a remodel calls for it, we coordinate directly with engineers, architects, designers, and surveyors — so you get one team accountable for the whole result, backed by the personalized follow-up we’re known for.
