About us

Personalized care, built into every project.

We’re a small, deeply local team led by a builder who grew up on these islands. Every project gets the personal follow-through the islands are known for.

Founder & owner

Meet Kaya Maraya Silkiss-Hero.

Kaya founded Kare Kontracting in 2007 with a simple goal: bring real, full-service contracting to the San Juan Islands. A proud biracial American woman originally from Lopez Island, she now leads the company from Friday Harbor — and has managed and improved real estate properties since she was twenty-three.

Raised on a farm near the Salish Sea by hardworking parents, she pairs hands-on building experience with an unusually wide background: degrees in equine psychotherapy, Native American studies, and chemical dependency counseling, plus certifications in welding, dentistry, and ropes-course facilitation. She has been featured on A&E’s Hoarders, taken on government contract work, and keeps strong relationships with local tradespeople across the islands.

Off the jobsite she’s a professional photographer, a Maine Coon breeder, and a vocal advocate for women’s empowerment. On every project, that shows up the same way — with integrity, ethics, creativity, and a real interest in the people behind the work.

What we stand for

Three things we get right, every job.

A team is only as good as its standards. These are ours — written down so we can be held to them.

i.

Care, in the name and the work.

Every site is treated like our own home — respected, cleaned up, communicated about. We don’t leave loose ends.

ii.

Team work, dream work.

One project lead, in-house trades, and a crew that’s been together for years. No finger-pointing, no surprises.

iii.

Done when you say it’s done.

We don’t consider a job complete until you’re 100% satisfied. The follow-up is part of the job, not a bonus.

A few of the places we work

From a kitchen on Orcas to a new build on the Peninsula.

Across seventeen years, our work has spanned every kind of build the Pacific Northwest can throw at us. Salt-air homes, off-grid cabins, vacation rentals, commercial spaces, and full-property remodels.
